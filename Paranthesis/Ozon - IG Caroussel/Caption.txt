What's Ozon?
Ozon is simply a platform providing the public an opportunity to discover, discuss, and share anything for the purpose of reaching a high level of environmental sustainability.

What's the purpose of Ozon?
Ozon exists as a platform connecting environmentally aware organizations/individuals and the general public to work as one with the goal of re-inventing the world into a more green world. Our main purpose is to become part of the solution to the climate crisis, thus contributing to the solution for an environmentally sustainable world.
